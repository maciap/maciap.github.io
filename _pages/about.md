I am a postdoctoral researcher at Scuola Normale Superiore. 

I defended my [doctoral thesis](https://aaltodoc.aalto.fi/server/api/core/bitstreams/ac917662-a4bc-4659-98a1-b3388da81e31/content) in January 2024 (Aalto University).

   * Interpretable machine learning and trustworthy AI. 
   * AI for science.
